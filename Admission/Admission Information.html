Admission Information

For admission into Royal Academy Secondary School a candidate must clear the following steps:

  • Admission Form needs to be collected from the Head office of Mirpur-1 Campus or any other branch
  • The form needs to be submitted with the necessary papers as mentioned in the Admission Form.
  • Students seeking admission for admission into classes from VI(five) to Class X(ten), students must appear for an admission test covering the English Language and Mathematics syllabus of the previous class attended.
  • Upon passing the test, the candidates will be given a Letter of Acceptance. Parents can then get their children admitted to the school.
  • All applicants for admission to the School must satisfy at least ONE of the following criteria:
    1. Any primary school graduation (or the equivalent in another school system)
    2. 10 years old by the end of the first month of the class five attendance
    3. 11 years old on the first day of the first class of attendance and has not been in school for at least one year
    4. Completion of a certificate or equivalent from a special needs program and has been in school for next 5 years
    5. All applicants must also meet the College's English language and Mathmatics proficiency requirement.
